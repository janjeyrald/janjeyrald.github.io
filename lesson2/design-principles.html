White Space and Clean Design

Shaklee

The website of Shaklee has a lot of white space in between the graphics, gutters and space between lines of type. According to the design principles it white space is a space where your can rest your eyes. It has enough space to make the website looks clean.

Contrast

AMD

The website of AMD shows a good contrast to it's headings, paragraph, foreground and background and it's images. It shows hierarchy in it's elements.

Rule of Thirds

The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints

The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints website applies the Rule of Thirds because if you will divide it to 9 equal parts you will see that Jesus Christ is at the intersection of those lines, being the most important compositional element in the website image.
